Birds

The WBRF is dedicated to protecting and conserving bird species worldwide. By connecting experts, researchers, and nature lovers, WBRF works to ensure a sustainable future where birds and biodiversity can thrive together.

Eagle

Eagles are powerful birds of prey known for their sharp eyesight and incredible flying abilities. They build large nests,hunters, preying on fish, small mammals , and other birds. They symbolize strength and freedom in many cultures around the world.

Interesting facts

Eagles can fly at altitudes of 10,000 to 20,000 feet. They typically weigh between 6.6 and 14 pounds and can reach a diving speed of over 100 mph.
